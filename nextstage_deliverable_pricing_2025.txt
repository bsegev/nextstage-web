======================================================
TIER RECOMMENDATIONS BY SCENARIO
======================================================

FOR AGGRESSIVE GROWTH (Target 30% more revenue):
→ Use HIGH TIER pricing across all deliverables
→ Focus on value-based selling and ROI demonstration
→ Target enterprise clients and well-funded startups

FOR MARKET PENETRATION (Compete on value):
→ Use HYBRID STRATEGY 
→ Freelancer-competitive on design/creative work
→ Consultant-competitive on strategy/tech work
→ Positions you as the "smart choice"

FOR STABLE GROWTH (Current positioning):
→ Stay with MEDIUM TIER (current pricing)
→ Selective increases on high-demand services
→ 15-20% annual price increases

FOR VOLUME STRATEGY (More clients, lower margins):
→ Use LOW TIER pricing
→ Focus on efficiency and standardized processes
→ Target cost-conscious startups and SMBs
